Welcome to **Oh My Computer Science**, a practical field guide to the most-used machine-learning algorithms, organized into eleven families — from supervised learning and deep learning through to graphs, vision, and reinforcement learning.

For every family you'll find a ranked table of the most popular algorithms, each with a short *best for* note and the fields where it's commonly applied. Where it matters, we also call out sensible **default choices** — the handful of methods that will get you most of the way there before you reach for something fancier. The rankings reflect practical popularity rather than theoretical purity: these are the algorithms working engineers actually reach for.

The site uses a warm **melange** color palette and a clean, **rust-doc-inspired** layout with a persistent sidebar and cards generated automatically from each chapter. The goal is a single screen you can scan to pick the right tool for the job.

Use the sidebar to jump to any chapter, or pick a card below.
